My Story. My Reasons.

Dear reader,

My story began over 10 years ago. I studied journalism, during my final year of university I started a blog. I fell in love with the design and development aspect of it. I would spend more time working on the blog's look than actual journalism work.

After graduation, the blog I created was doing well. It received awards from major magazines and people were actually reading it.

Everything seemed so unreal.

However, a year after, I got unwell, my mental health was deteriorating and I gave up on the work I had spent almost two years creating.

Everything fell apart, I didn't know how to pick myself up again.

A few years after my first psychotic episode, I finally found the motivation to move forward again. I wanted to work with young kids. I wanted to change lives.

I studied to become a teacher. This is when my thoughts started wandering. What if, in the future I open a coding school for young children? This thought has remained with me ever since. It has become my life goal.

So, if you are wondering why I want to become a developer? My motivation is my love for coding and my reason is to make my dream a reality.
